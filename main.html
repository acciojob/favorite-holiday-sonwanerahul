MAHABALESHWAR

Mahabaleshwar , a popular hill station in Maharashtra's Western Ghats, is a stunning, high-altitude destination renowned for its lush greenery, pleasant climate, and expansive strawberry farms. Once the summer capital of the Bombay Presidency, this scenic town offers breathtaking valley views from points like Arthur's Seat and attractions such as Venna Lake and the ancient Mahabaleshwar Temple. It serves as a major tourist hub, especially favored for trekking, boating, and its refreshing, cool weather.
